# Favorite Albums of 2024

## 10. Stardust 

{{< spotify "spotify:album:0h3LYQns7VLlsMW7392Z1X" >}}

Stardust is a very strange combo of rap and hyperpop which has grown on me the more I have listened to it.

## 9. Love & Ponystep

{{< spotify "spotify:album:2g5xTWYxh5RkalkOc2Rxds" >}}

If you told me I would have two dubstep albums and more particularly brostep albums for 2025 I would not have believed you. This one is for some reason also a MLP album for some reason and to be honest it kinds of holds back the album. It's a bit distracting to have an absolute banger have lyrics about shaking your tail. 

## 8. Hurry Up Tomorrow

{{< spotify "spotify:album:3OxfaVgvTxUTy7276t7SPU" >}}

A solid conclusion to the Weeknd's trilogy.

## 7. TRON: Ares (Original Motion Picture Soundtrack)

{{< spotify "spotify:album:47pjW3XDPW99NShtkeewxl" >}}

This is my favorite Tron soundtrack which is impressive considering how much I love the other two.

## 6. F*CK U SKRILLEX YOU THINK UR ANDY WARHOL BUT UR NOT!! <3

{{< spotify "spotify:album:784znju28Nz5zSuRuthPcO" >}}

This is Skrillex's best album. It has all the things that made him great condensed into a single album that is fun from start to finish. And I guess since he's no longer super popular it's cool to say he makes good music now even though it's always been good.

## 5. Foxes in the Snow

{{< spotify "spotify:album:6OgNgnMcqhcDw01qXlw8el" >}}

I didn't know Jason Isbell got divorced until listening to this album which was a sad way to learn about that. Both albums from that divorce are good and worth listening to.

## 4. Lonesome Drifter

{{< spotify "spotify:album:64lDGn2XUSnXAncXh9Zzow" >}}

This is my favorite Charley Crockett album. It has a combination of all the things he has done over the years and combines it into a single well paced album.

## 3. LUX

{{< spotify "spotify:album:3SUEJULSGgBDG1j4GQhfYY" >}}

I have no idea what most of the lyrics are but this is one of the best orchestrated albums.

## 2. Snipe Hunter

{{< spotify "spotify:album:00rssxYxFqqdbuO1lz1Gxa" >}}

Such a fun album with several over the top songs. I also can't believe this has the first studio recording of Nose on the Grindstone.

## 1. I Love my Computer

{{< spotify "spotify:album:77CZUF57sYqgtznUe3OikQ" >}}


I Love my Computer feels so unique and it's own things while borrowing heavily from the EDM that came before it. The lyrics are genuine and it's a fun listen from beginning to end.
