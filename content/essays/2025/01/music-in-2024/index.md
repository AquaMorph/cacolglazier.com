# Favorite Albums of 2024

## 10. Drink the River

{{< spotify "spotify:album:66w66TUwHavAnh3f0cOQdj" >}}

Solid song writing and one of my favorite folk/country albums of the year.

## 9. Did you know that there's a tunnel under Ocean Blvd

{{< spotify "spotify:album:5HOHne1wzItQlIYmLXLYfZ" >}}

Lana Del Rey has always been one of my favorites but I didn't click with this album initially. Upon listening to it again later it's actually one of her better albums.

## 8. Fountain Baby

{{< spotify "spotify:album:3dKdkVeR6hPjAOH5K2nYTh" >}}

This album is goofy but I really enjoy it well except for the bits about astrology which is just stupid.

## 7. The Great American Bar Scene

{{< spotify "spotify:album:1U0Z7QjSzlg3gMeUOuUldz" >}}

Very solid Zach Bryan but I prefer his other ones.

## 6. $10 Cowboy

{{< spotify "spotify:album:29H8rVCQG9uXLiT9hlH5za" >}}

Charlie Crockett does not miss and $10 Cowboy is an impressive showing in a streak of so many great albums.

## 5. Imaginal Disk

{{< spotify "spotify:album:4HTy9WFTYooRjE9giTmzAF" >}}

An amazing follow up to the first groundbreaking album.

## 4. Mahashmashana

{{< spotify "spotify:album:2oJHtuXrCJ0KMH5GrC6Qdw" >}}

One of the best produced Father John Misty albums although for some reason it didn't stick with me as much as other albums.

## 3. Passage Du Desir

{{< spotify "spotify:album:71On7h3S7yH5D0Td6YNw1t" >}}

This albums feels like a spiritual successor to Sailor's Guide to Earth which is my favorite album. It bounces between fun and absolutely gut wrenching songs. I do forgive Sturgill for using legos instead of LEGO bricks.

## 2. Critterland

{{< spotify "spotify:album:3Fcxe3eYAh7rDhIUlx7Uxd" >}}

This was a very good your for country music and this was my favorite country album of the year. The live version of it is also fantastic.

## 1. Brat

{{< spotify "spotify:album:2lIZef4lzdvZkiiCzvPKj7" >}}

This was the meme album of the summer but it was also the best.
